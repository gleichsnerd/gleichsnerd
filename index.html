A Brief History of gleichsnerd

Hi there! My name is Adam Gleichsner, and I'm a frontend engineer and collector of hobbies based out of Columbus, Ohio, USA.

By day, I'm the tech lead for frontend development at Paxata, a company that specializes in building the future of data preparation by putting the power in the hands of the business analysts. In non-PR lingo? I write a vast amount of code in a well-weathered codebase while helping to educate/mentor my teammates and drive our tech stack towards the future, bit by bit.

Currently, my technical interests lie all across UI/UX/Frontend-land along with occasional oddball curiosities like WebGL/ThreeJS and Raspberry Pi projects. I've previously worked as a full-stack engineer so I try to stay sharp by building end-to-end eclectic and (hopefully) wholesome hobby projects that I can't wait to share.

Besides being a software engineer and the holder of the coveted family alias, gleichsnerd, I'm also an avid camper, dabbling film photographer, occasional sculptor, and general purveyor of internet tomfoolery. I'm also prone to the occasional Netflix binge, love decompressing to video games, and have a small family of pets that my fiancÃ©e and I spoil without abandon.
